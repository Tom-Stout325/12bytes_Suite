To add amortization tables to an existing vehicle, do this after applying the patch and running migrations:
Go to the vehicle’s Annual Record page for the year you want.
Open the annual record for that vehicle, or create one if it does not exist yet.
Leave Annual Interest Paid blank if you want MoneyPro to calculate interest automatically.
Fill in the loan fields:
purchase date
first payment date if you have it
loan amount
annual interest rate
number of payments
Check Generate or refresh amortization schedule.
Save the annual record.
What happens next:
MoneyPro creates the amortization rows for that vehicle loan.
It totals the interest for the selected calendar year.
It calculates the business portion using that year’s business-use percent.
For a vehicle that already exists, you do not need to recreate the vehicle itself. You just add the loan details through the annual record and save with generation turned on.
A couple of practical notes:
If you already typed a manual annual interest amount, that manual amount takes priority over the generated amount.
If you want the system-generated number, clear out the manual interest field first.
If the vehicle has multiple years, you can create annual records for each year and the generated schedule will feed the year-specific interest totals.
